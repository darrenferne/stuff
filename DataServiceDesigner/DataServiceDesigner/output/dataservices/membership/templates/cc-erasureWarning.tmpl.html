The purpose of this utility is to delete, or pseudonymise to the extent deletion is not applicable in the circumstances, Personal Data, as defined in the 2015 EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), that is stored in your Brady licensed product(s). Personal Data includes any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person including data or information where a person can be identified, directly or indirectly.   Deletion includes a complete erasure of direct identifiers and in some cases, certain indirect identifiers that could combine to reveal a person’s identity. Pseudonymisation involves obscuring, direct identifiers and in some cases, certain indirect identifiers that could combine to reveal a person’s identity.
